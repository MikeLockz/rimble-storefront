A transaction failure modal should let the user know their transaction has failed and what to do next.

```js is=react-live hideCode
// import { Modal, Card, Box, Blockie, Flex, Icon, Text, Link, Heading, Button } from "rimble-ui";

<Card p={0} borderRadius={1} mb={4}>
  <Box height="4px" bg="danger" borderRadius={["1rem 1rem 0 0"]} />
  <Flex
    justifyContent="space-between"
    alignItems="center"
    borderBottom={1}
    borderColor="near-white"
    p={[3, 4]}
    pb={3}
  >
    <Icon name="Warning" color="danger" aria-label="Warning" />
    <Heading textAlign="center" as="h1" fontSize={[2, 3]} px={[3, 0]}>
      Buying 10 BAT failed
    </Heading>
    <Link>
      <Icon
        name="Close"
        color="moon-gray"
        aria-label="Close and cancel connection"
      />
    </Link>
  </Flex>
  <Text p={[3, 4]}>
    We couldnâ€™t confirm your purchase because your transaction fee proved too
    low. Based on the network right now, we recommend trying again with a
    transaction fee of at least 0.005 ETH.
  </Text>
  <Flex
    p={[3, 4]}
    borderTop={1}
    borderColor="near-white"
    justifyContent="flex-end"
    flexDirection={["column", "row"]}
    alignItems="center"
  >
    <Button.Outline mr={[0, 3]} mb={[2, 0]} width={["100%", "auto"]}>
      View on Etherscan
    </Button.Outline>
    <Button width={["100%", "auto"]}>Try again</Button>
  </Flex>
</Card>
```
